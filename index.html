About Us

We opened our first BreadCrumbs Bakery in Boston's South End in 2000 with one goal: to make the world a sweeter place through warm smiles, gooey sticky buns, and irresistible BLTs. Every day we pinch ourselves that we get to do what we love - share awesome food and welcoming hospitality - with everyone who walks through our doors and visits us here.

Led by James Beard Award-winning pastry chef + co-owner Joanne Chang, Flour Bakery now has nine locations in Boston + Cambridge. We offer buttery breakfast pastries; soft + chewy cookies; luscious pies; gorgeous cakes; and fresh, made-to-order sandwiches, soups, and salads - all prepared daily by our dedicated team.

Our Awesome Cakes

Our Chocolate Drip Cake is layered and frosted, that features a decorative layer of chocolate ganache or glaze that is poured over the top edge, allowing it to drip down the sides in a visually appealing effect. It's not only visually appealing it's absolutely delicious!

Our Carrot Cake is a classic a beloved sweet and moist cake with a unique flavor profile. It's a spice cake, similar to a spice cake, but with added moisture and subtle flavor from the carrots.


Our German Chocolate Cake is a delightful blend of rich chocolate cake, sweet and slightly caramel-like coconut-pecan frosting, with just a hint of nutty flavor from the pecans. The sweetness of the frosting is balanced by the chocolate cake layers.
